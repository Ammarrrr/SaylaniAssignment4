about us

Renowned name in Automotive Industry
  SNA Industries a renowned name in the automotive industry was established in the year 1990 by a young entrepreneur who had experienced in manufacturing, marketing, and business management. Initially, the company was engaged in the manufacturing and marketing of brake pipelines and hydraulic brake hoses for the aftermarket. The company grew rapidly from a humble beginning and had captured most of the country's market share. SNA has become a very strong name not only in the aftermarket but also in the OEM area. To increased activity and profitability. Maximum efforts were made to increase the product range.
